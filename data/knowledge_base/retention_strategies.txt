# Telecom Customer Retention Strategies

## Contract-Based Retention

### Month-to-Month Customers
Month-to-month customers are the highest churn risk group. They have no commitment and can leave at any time.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Offer a 15-20% discount on monthly charges for signing a 1-year contract
- Provide a one-time loyalty credit of $50-$100 for contract upgrade
- Bundle additional services (security, backup) at no extra cost for 3 months
- Waive installation fees for service upgrades
- Offer a price-lock guarantee for 12 months

### One-Year Contract Customers
These customers show moderate commitment but may leave at renewal.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Contact 60 days before contract expiration with a renewal offer
- Offer a 10% discount for upgrading to a 2-year contract
- Provide free premium channel package for 6 months on renewal
- Send personalized thank-you communication acknowledging loyalty

### Two-Year Contract Customers
These are the most stable customers. Focus on maintaining satisfaction.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Provide periodic service quality checks
- Offer early upgrade options for new services
- Include in VIP customer events and promotions
- Assign dedicated account manager for accounts over $100/month

## Billing and Payment Retention

### High Monthly Charges (Above $70/month)
Customers with high monthly charges are price-sensitive and may seek cheaper alternatives.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Review current plan for unnecessary services and optimize billing
- Offer a competitive rate match if customer mentions competitor pricing
- Provide a temporary 3-month discount of 15-25% while reviewing options
- Suggest bundling services for overall cost reduction
- Offer autopay discount of $5-$10/month

### Electronic Check Payment Users
Customers paying by electronic check show higher churn rates than credit card or bank transfer users.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Offer $5/month discount for switching to autopay with credit card
- Explain the convenience of automatic payments
- Highlight late fee avoidance benefits of autopay
- Provide paper billing option if customer prefers physical statements
