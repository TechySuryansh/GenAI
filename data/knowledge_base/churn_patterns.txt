# Customer Demographics and Churn Risk Patterns

## Tenure-Based Risk Analysis

### New Customers (0-12 months)
New customers are the highest risk segment. They have not yet built loyalty or habit with the service.

**Key Observations:**
- 45% of all churn occurs within the first 12 months
- Customers who pass the 12-month mark are 60% less likely to churn
- Month-to-month new customers churn at 3x the rate of contracted new customers

**Intervention Timing:**
- First contact at 30 days: Welcome call and satisfaction check
- Second contact at 90 days: Service optimization review
- Third contact at 6 months: Loyalty offer for contract commitment
- Fourth contact at 10 months: Pre-renewal retention offer

### Mid-Tenure Customers (12-36 months)
These customers have shown initial commitment but may be evaluating alternatives.

**Key Observations:**
- Price sensitivity increases as initial promotional rates expire
- Service quality expectations rise with tenure
- Competitors actively target customers at contract renewal points

### Long-Tenure Customers (36+ months)
Loyal customers who rarely churn but provide the highest lifetime value.

**Key Observations:**
- Low churn risk but high impact if they do leave
- Often under-serviced because they're assumed to be "safe"
- May be paying legacy rates that are higher than current market rates

## Demographic Patterns

### Senior Citizens
- Higher churn rate despite lower overall percentage of customer base
- Often face usability challenges with digital services
- Value personalized human support over automated systems
- Respond well to simplified plans and dedicated support lines

### Customers Without Partners/Dependents
- More likely to make quick switching decisions
- Less anchored by family service bundles
- More responsive to individual-focused offers and flexibility
- Value contract flexibility over bundle discounts

### Customers With Partners/Dependents
- More stable due to shared services and family plans
- Price-sensitive due to overall household expenses
- Respond well to family bundle offers and multi-line discounts
- Value reliability and consistent service quality
