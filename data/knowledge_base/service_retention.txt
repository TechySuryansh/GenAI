# Service-Based Retention Strategies

## Internet Service Retention

### Fiber Optic Customers
Fiber optic customers pay premium prices and expect premium service. They churn when they feel the price doesn't match the value.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Offer speed upgrade at no additional cost for 6 months
- Bundle cybersecurity protection and cloud backup free for 3 months
- Provide a dedicated support line for fiber customers
- Offer whole-home WiFi solution at discounted rate
- Match or beat competitor fiber pricing with a loyalty guarantee

### DSL Customers
DSL customers are often price-conscious. They may churn to competitors offering fiber at similar prices.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Offer discounted upgrade path to fiber optic
- Provide free router upgrade for improved performance
- Bundle phone service at reduced rate
- Highlight reliability and coverage advantages of current service

### No Internet Service Customers
Customers without internet may have limited engagement with the company.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Offer introductory internet package at promotional rate
- Bundle internet with existing phone service for overall savings
- Provide free trial period for internet service

## Support and Security Services

### Customers Without Online Security
Lack of security services indicates lower engagement and higher churn risk.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Offer free 3-month trial of online security suite
- Educate customer on cyber threats and protection benefits
- Bundle security with existing services at 50% discount
- Highlight identity theft protection features

### Customers Without Tech Support
Customers without tech support may become frustrated with technical issues and churn.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Provide one free tech support call to demonstrate value
- Offer tech support add-on at $5/month instead of standard $10/month
- Include remote troubleshooting capability
- Bundle with device protection for comprehensive coverage

## Streaming Service Retention

### Customers with Streaming Services
Streaming customers use more bandwidth and are more engaged.

**Recommended Actions:**
- Offer premium streaming package upgrades at loyalty pricing
- Provide bandwidth guarantee for streaming quality
- Bundle multiple streaming platforms for discount
- Highlight exclusive content partnerships
